Questions

Frequently asked

No account needed at all. Install the extension and start using it immediately. Your first 3 projects are stored locally in your browser — completely private, no sign-in required. You only need a Google account to unlock cloud sync and collaboration features on the Premium plan.
FlowLane supports all Chromium-based browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, and Vivaldi. Any browser that supports the Chrome Web Store extension format will work.
On the free tier, your data stays on your device — only you can access it. On Premium, data is synced to our secure cloud with strict access rules that ensure only you (and collaborators you explicitly invite) can access your boards. We never sell or share your data.
On Premium, you can invite collaborators to any project by email. Each collaborator gets a role: Admin (full control), Editor (can create/edit cards), or Viewer (read-only). Changes sync in real time — everyone sees updates instantly. You can also see who's currently online via live presence indicators.
Premium is $3.99/mo on the monthly plan or $39.99/yr on the annual plan (just $3.33/mo — save 16%). Both plans include unlimited projects, cloud sync across devices, real-time collaboration, and role-based access. You can cancel anytime directly from within the extension.
Yes, cancel anytime directly from within the extension — no hoops, no emails required. If you cancel, you'll stay on Premium until the end of your billing period. After that, you'll revert to the free plan. You can export all your data as JSON or CSV at any time before downgrading.
Yes! The web app is live at app.flowlanekanban.com — use FlowLane on any browser, any device, with no extension to install. The web app is exclusively available to Premium subscribers. Sign in with your Google account and your Premium subscription will be verified automatically. Free-tier users can use the Chrome extension instead.
